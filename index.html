Phase 0: Foundation

Problem & Solution Validation

Main Goal

Prove the existence of the problem and the value of the solution (Problem/Solution Fit).

GTM & BizDev

[PROJECT 1] Customer Development

Conduct 30-50 interviews. Result: Understanding of 'pains' and willingness to pay.

[PROJECT 2] Document Rework

Rework Pitch Deck, Business & Financial models. Result: Convincing documents for investors.

[PROJECT 3] 'Smoke Screen'

Launch a landing page, start collecting emails. Result: A list of early adopters.

Product & Tech

[PROJECT 1] Technical CustDev

Participate in 10-15 tech interviews. Result: Understanding of tech limitations.

[PROJECT 2] MVP Architecture

Design the architecture. Result: Technical specification for the MVP.

[PROJECT 3] Prototype Creation

Develop a clickable prototype (Figma). Result: A tool for testing.

Foundation & People

[PROJECT] Legal & Admin Setup

Register company, prepare contracts. Result: A legally sound operational entity.

[PROJECT] Co-founder Agreement

Solidify roles, equity, and vesting. Result: Clear alignment for the founding team.

Transition Criteria

  • >20 Letters of Intent (LoI)
  • Financial model shows break-even point
